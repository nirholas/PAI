PAI 1.0 is publicly available. Boot a private AI workstation from any USB drive — no installation, no cloud, no tracking.

## What's included

- **Ollama** with Llama 3.2 and Mistral models pre-loaded
- **Flash** app to write PAI to a USB drive from any OS
- **Verified boot** with signed image checksums
- **Full disk encryption** with LUKS2
- Supports x86_64 and ARM64 (including Apple Silicon via Asahi)

## Download

Grab the image from the [Flash](/apps/flash) app on this page, or download directly from the releases page.
