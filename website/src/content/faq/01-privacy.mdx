---
question: "Is this really private?"
order: 1
---

Yes — genuinely private, not "trust us" private.

PAI runs entirely in RAM and never touches your computer's hard drive. The host OS is never mounted. All AI inference happens locally via Ollama; your prompts never leave your machine. When you shut down, RAM is cleared and no forensic trace remains — no chat history, no downloaded files, no browser cookies, nothing.

The network stack adds further protection: your MAC address is randomized on every boot, the firewall denies all incoming connections, and Ollama binds only to `localhost`. Even if someone is on the same Wi-Fi network, they cannot reach your AI session.

For maximum privacy, enable Tor with `sudo pai-privacy on` to route all outbound traffic through the Tor network.
