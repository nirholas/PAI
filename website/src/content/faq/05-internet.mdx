---
question: "Does it need internet?"
order: 5
---

No — PAI is designed to work fully offline.

The AI inference engine (Ollama) runs entirely on your hardware. Once you've downloaded a model, you can disconnect from the internet and keep chatting. Your prompts never leave your machine.

**What needs internet (first time only):**
- Downloading AI models via `ollama pull <model>` — models are 1–8 GB each
- Accessing web content in Firefox

**What never needs internet:**
- Running AI conversations with already-downloaded models
- Accessing the PAI chat interface at `localhost:8080`
- Everything else in PAI's default workflow

**With persistence enabled:** download your models once, save them, and they're available offline on every subsequent boot.

**Air-gapped operation:** if you need complete network isolation, PAI works perfectly with the Wi-Fi/Ethernet physically disconnected. The firewall also supports this: all AI services are localhost-only by default.
