PAI ships without any pre-bundled models to keep the ISO small (~912 MB). You download models on first use.

Any model supported by Ollama works — that's hundreds of models:

| Model | Size | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| `phi3:mini` | ~2 GB | Fast, low RAM, capable |
| `llama3.2:3b` | ~2 GB | Fast, good quality |
| `llama3.1:8b` | ~5 GB | High quality, balanced |
| `mistral:7b` | ~4 GB | Fast, strong reasoning |
| `gemma2:9b` | ~5 GB | Google's model, great quality |
| `codellama:7b` | ~4 GB | Code-focused tasks |
| `deepseek-coder-v2` | ~9 GB | Advanced coding |

To pull a model:

```bash
ollama pull llama3.2
```

Or through the PAI chat interface at `localhost:8080`.

**Hardware guidance:**
- 4 GB RAM: use `phi3:mini` or `llama3.2:3b`
- 8 GB RAM: use `llama3.1:8b` or `mistral:7b`  
- 16 GB+ RAM: use larger 13B+ models

**Pre-bundling roadmap:** a future release will include a small model (e.g., Phi-3 Mini) baked into the ISO for instant offline use without any downloads.
