# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the PAI community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the maintainers listed in [MAINTAINERS.md](MAINTAINERS.md). All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
